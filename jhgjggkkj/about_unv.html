Intellectual Property Right Cell

ABOUT

Intellectual property (IP) could be a category of property that incorporates intangible manifestations of the human judgment skills. The IP Cell of MAKAUT points to ensure IP rights. The main purpose of intellectual property law is to encourage the creation of a wide assortment of intellectual products. To attain this, the law gives individuals and businesses property rights to the data and intellectual products they make, as a rule for a constrained time. This gives an economic incentive for their creation because it allows people to benefit from the information and intellectual goods they create and allows them to protect their ideas and prevent copying. These financial motivations are anticipated to fortify advancement and contribute to the technological advancements of nations, which depends on the extent of protection granted to innovators.
Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), formerly known as West Bengal University of Technology (WBUT), is a public state university located in Kolkata, West Bengal. The University operates from its main campus in Haringhata, Nadia and its administrative office in Bidhannagar. It affiliates 197 colleges and is the largest affiliating University in India. A leader in the sphere of Technical Education, MAKAUT continuously strives to upgrade Technical Education with the gracious aid of the Government of West Bengal, UGC and AICTE. Today, the University provides degrees in Engineering, Management as well as a plethora of other professional areas through its affiliated institutions and University in-house departments. It is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and ranks 165 among all Engineering colleges in India according to the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) in 2019. The University is offering financial and other supports for the In-house students and faculty members for patenting their innovative ideas, designs, products etc.
