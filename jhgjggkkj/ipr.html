Intellectual Property Right Cell

Guidelines

This IP Policy is binding in all matters related to IPR at MAKAUT. However, changes may be brought to the policy from time to time due to the changing IP scenarios which may be done either per case basis or in whole. The IPR Cell will address such specified cases by using this IP policy document as the guidelines. IPR Cell of MAKAUT will be responsible for processing all IPR related matters, viz., any intellectual property generated out of any intellectual effort.

Scope

This IP Policy applies to all faculty members, employees (regular or contractual) and students of MAKAUT,WB. Every member must be aware of their rights and respect the rights of others about intellectual property. Any special case can be dealt with by IPR Cell with due approval of the managing committee.
